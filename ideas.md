<idea>
**Design Movement:** Clinical Brutalism meets Silicon Valley Precision
**Core Principles:**
1. High-contrast light theme — white/off-white base with deep navy text, zero ambiguity
2. Asymmetric column layouts — content never perfectly centered, always offset to create tension and interest
3. Data-first visual language — every section features a live-looking UI mockup, diagram, or metric
4. Borders as structure — thin 1px lines delineate sections and cards, no heavy shadows

**Color Philosophy:** White (#ffffff) and near-white (#f8fafc) as the dominant canvas. Deep navy (#0a0f1e) for primary text and dark sections. Electric cyan (#00c8e8) as the single accent — used sparingly for CTAs, highlights, and diagram connectors. Bright blue (#2563eb) for interactive elements. The restraint creates authority.

**Layout Paradigm:** Alternating full-bleed sections — light/dark/light/dark — with content that bleeds edge-to-edge on dark sections. On light sections, content is offset left with diagrams/mockups floating right. Navigation is sticky with a frosted glass effect.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Animated constellation/node graph in hero (matching the Reqilo logo motif)
2. Live-looking dashboard mockups embedded in workflow sections
3. Thin cyan connector lines between workflow steps

**Interaction Philosophy:** Scroll-triggered reveals, hover states that illuminate cards with a subtle cyan glow, tab-switching between Supplier/Buyer views.

**Animation:** Entrance animations at 200-300ms ease-out. Hero constellation nodes animate on load. Workflow steps reveal sequentially with 60ms stagger. Dark sections have a subtle parallax.

**Typography System:** Space Grotesk (headings, 700/800) + Inter (body, 400/500). Labels in Inter 600 uppercase tracked wide. Metric numbers in Space Grotesk 800.

## Selected Approach: Precision Engineering — Dark Sections with Light Canvas

This approach best achieves the brief: Vercel-style tech-forward aesthetic adapted for healthcare, lighter theme overall, high contrast, simplified workflow diagrams, and a design that connects both the healthcare and computing spaces. The alternating light/dark rhythm mirrors the dual-sided platform (Supplier/Buyer), and the node/constellation motif ties directly to the Reqilo logo.
